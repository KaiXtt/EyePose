EyePose: DCIR-Based Gesture Demonstration

This page presents synchronized visual comparisons among Differential Channel Impulse Responses (DCIR), ground-truth hand-pose annotations derived from the MediaPipe, and system-predicted keypoint trajectories(2X Slower), covering several representative gesture categories. The visualization aims to facilitate academic evaluation and provide a supplementary demonstration of the EyePose system.
